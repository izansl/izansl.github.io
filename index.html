About Me

I am a passionate Game Developer & Designer, currently in my final year at CITM (UPC). Since childhood, I have admired video games, initially as a spectator watching my older brother play, and over time, I have embraced the opportunity to become a creator and pursue a career in the industry.

Creativity has always been one of my strongest qualities, and I channel it into every project I work on. I specialize in Game Design, with a particular focus on narrative and gameplay mechanics. I am especially inspired by games that leave a lasting emotional impact on players, and I strive daily to develop experiences capable of achieving the same.

Beyond design, I am also interested in other aspects of development, particularly 3D modeling, which I practice as a hobby. This skill has allowed me to contribute to collaborative projects and support my teammates effectively.

My goal is to continue growing as a creator, combining creativity, technical skills, and passion to craft video games that truly connect with players.
